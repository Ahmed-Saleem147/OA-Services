Full capability set

53 features across 10 modules.

Tap any module to see what it covers.

Centralised real-time operations hub. All departmental data in one place, updated live.
Role-based departmental views. Management, HR, Finance and Operations each see their tailored dashboard.
Cloud-connected with live data sync. Every module pushes data in real time, with no manual refresh needed.
Single sign-on access. Secure, role-managed login for all authorised users.
Customisable KPIs and metrics. Configure the indicators that matter most per department.
Mobile and desktop accessible. Full functionality on any device, browser-based, with no installation required.
Executive performance snapshot. A top-level view of all key organisational metrics, updated live.
Revenue and pipeline tracking. Live view of booked revenue, active sales pipeline and conversion probability.
Division performance comparison. Side-by-side comparison of division contributions against targets.
Operating margin and YTD tracking. Real-time margin visibility with year-to-date trend analysis.
Priority alerts and action items. Critical items surface automatically with one-click authorisation workflows.
Top performer recognition. Track quota attainment by executive and team, and identify top performers instantly.
Supply chain and operational alerts. Automatic flags for delays, risks or disruptions affecting targets.
Budget vs. actuals tracking. See allocation utilisation in real time, drilling into payroll, OPEX or capital reserves.
Real-time expenditure monitoring. Every spend category tracked live against approved budgets.
Disbursement ledger. Full audit trail of every disbursement, from leases and infrastructure to travel and marketing.
Approval and authorisation workflows. Finance approves contractor budgets and spend directly in the dashboard.
Administrative penalty and tax tracking. Compliance-related financial obligations tracked and flagged automatically.
Quarterly and annual financial reporting. Auto-generated reports covering the full financial period, exportable to Excel.
Headcount tracking and recruitment pipeline. Monitor open roles, candidates in progress and hiring timelines.
Open requisitions and time-to-hire metrics. Know exactly how long each hire is taking and where it stands.
Payroll disbursement overview. Live payroll tracking per quarter, aligned with attendance and budget.
Staff onboarding and equipment requisitions. Track and authorise new hire hardware and onboarding tasks.
New hire allocation tracking. Equipment and resource allocations for incoming staff, managed in one place.
Live biometric clock-in and clock-out data. Face recognition and fingerprint data feeds directly into the dashboard.
Late arrivals and absenteeism flagging. Automatic alerts for patterns of lateness or unexplained absence.
Overtime tracking. Overtime hours logged and surfaced automatically, with no manual calculation.
Auto-generated attendance reports. Daily, weekly and monthly reports generated without manual input.
Payroll pre-population from attendance. Attendance data used to pre-fill payroll records, eliminating double-entry.
Real-time entry and exit logs. Every person entering or exiting is logged with time, date and identity.
Restricted zone access tracking. Know exactly who accessed sensitive areas and when.
Breach and forced entry alerts. Instant push notifications to management when access rules are violated.
Full tamper-proof audit trail. Complete access history stored securely and retrievable at any time.
Electronic lock and barrier gate status. Live status of all controlled doors, gates and barriers on the dashboard.
Real-time website traffic tracking. Daily, weekly and monthly visitor data, covering sources, pages and behaviour.
Lead and enquiry monitoring. Track how many enquiries your website generates and where they drop off.
Digital campaign performance. Link campaigns to revenue and see which channels drive real results.
Website-to-sales pipeline connection. Website leads flow directly into the sales dashboard for end-to-end tracking.
One-click Excel export. Any dashboard table, ledger or report exports to Excel instantly, formatted and clean.
Two-way sync with existing spreadsheets. Link your current budget spreadsheets so changes reflect in both directions.
Automated stakeholder reporting. Set up weekly or monthly auto-reports sent as Excel files from live data.
Historical data import. Import years of existing spreadsheet data for trend analysis and forecasting.
No double-entry required. Existing workflows are preserved, so staff continue using Excel without disruption.
Fleet operational status tracking. Live fleet availability and operational status, with weekly trend comparison.
Daily OPEX monitoring. Operational expenditure tracked daily against projections.
Active facilities overview. All facilities tracked as active, under maintenance or pending inspection.
Asset status ledger. Every asset logged with type, location, valuation and last audit date.
Critical maintenance alerts. Pending maintenance items surface automatically with estimated costs.
ICT project registry tracking. All digital projects registered and tracked for NITA compliance.
NITA Bill 2025 compliance-ready. Built in alignment with Ghana's NITA licensing and operational framework.
Secure cloud data storage. All data stored with encryption and role-based access controls.
Full audit trails across all modules. Every action, change and access event logged and retrievable.
Role-based permissions. Staff see only the data relevant to their role, and nothing more.

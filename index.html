Help Centre

Common Questions

Quick answers to help you understand how working with us looks in practice.

How long does installation take?
Installation timelines vary based on project scope and complexity. A simple attendance system might take a few days, while a multi-site deployment could take several weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline during our initial consultation.
Can systems be customized to our needs?
Absolutely. All our systems are fully customizable. We work with you to understand your workflows and configure every element to match your requirements — from access permissions to reporting formats.
Do you provide support after installation?
Yes. Ongoing support is a core part of what we offer. We provide training, technical assistance, maintenance, and system updates throughout our partnership with you.
What areas do you support?
We provide support for device setup, dashboard usage, attendance management, and general system maintenance. Our team is available to help at every stage of the process.
